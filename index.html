About me

I am a software generalist who focuses mostly on the web. Whether I am serving an internal or external user, I aim to simplify the user's life by hiding the complexity of the world behind an interface.

I have had the privilege of leading the development of multiple impactful products from user interviews to production. Most of my experience includes building scalable web applications, internal pattern libraries, and productivity tools. I tend to focus on coding quality & standards, reusability & composition, and performance.

I value and thrive in teams that foster a culture of sharing actionable feedback, a bias for action, and the freedom to experiment.

Before starting my career in tech I was a touring musician. The four years I spent playing in a band, and touring the country was a dream, but I'm much happier leading a more simple life.

I enjoy spending time outdoors, traveling, going to concerts, and trying new recipies with my daughter and girlfriend.

Whether it's the daily Wordle, Leetcode, new tech, learning a new song on the drums, I always take time out of my day to challenge myself. I am a forever learner, and cannot image that will ever change!
